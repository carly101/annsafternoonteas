In House Afternoon Tea
What to expect

When having an afternoon tea for 2 you will get a choice of 4 different sandwich fillings and 6 mini cakes and 2 scones. For 6 people you will get the choice of 6 different sandwich fillings 18 mini cakes and 6 scones. All of these include pots of tea , coffee , water and fizzy drinks.

Card Title

When you arrive at the cafe you will be seated at one of the decorated tables. You will then be asked who wants tea, coffee, pop or water from this you will be given coffee and tea pots to suit what you have asked for. Following this afternoon tea stand will be brought out to you with sandwiches and cakes which you have chosen from the list. Then you is left to do is sit back and relax
