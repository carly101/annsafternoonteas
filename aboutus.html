About us
  • free_breakfastThe Cafe
    Ann's cafe and catering is a family run business in the heart of norton canes. The cafe sell many selections of everyday foods including all day breakfasts and home cook lunches. We also now provide afternoon teas for all occasions. You can sit in or take out eaither way you are guaranteed a warm welcome.
  • free_breakfastAnn
    Ann is the manager/ owner of Ann's cafe and catering. She has been working with food for over 15 years. After coming out of the retail industry she had a short break and then decided to purse her love in food and buy a cafe. You'll find Ann in the front of the shop taking orders in the back preparing orders and out delving the orders. You will also get a warm and friendly smile from Ann and her team.
  • free_breakfastLesley
    Lesley is the mother of Ann and the other manger of the cafe. Lesley has over 20 years of working with food as before the cafe she was catering for birthdays, christening, meeting and sit down weddings. Lesley still does all this catering today. You'll find Lesley taking orders out the front but also she is our resident sponge bob as she loves being on the griddle cooking the food.
