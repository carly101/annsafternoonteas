Afternoon tea's

Aternoon Tea's were imtroduced in Britain in the early 1840s.People classed it as a mini meal to keep the hunger at bay untill an evening which was at 8pm. An Afternoon Tea is composed of sandwiches which are cut into fingers. You then get a selection of scones with Jam and clotted cream , small sweet pastries and cakes.
